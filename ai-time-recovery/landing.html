Usually, yes. We try to use free tiers where possible (e.g., Zapier Free, ChatGPT Free), but depending on volume, you may need basic subscriptions ($20-$50/mo) to keep the tools running. We will clarify this during the Audit Call.

What happens if something breaks later?

We provide video SOPs so you can manage minor issues. Since we don't charge a retainer, we don't offer ongoing maintenance. However, the systems we build are designed to be "low code" and stable.

Can you build a custom app for my staff?

No. That falls under custom software development. We specialize in connecting existing tools (like your Email, Calendar, and CRM) to automate data flow.
